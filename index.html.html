About

Community-first impact investing.

Black Harbor Investments deploys capital directly into underserved communities using deep local research, a five-step circular economy framework, and our technology platform Communivest — making impact investing accessible to everyone, with no minimum investment required.

Traditional ESG funds target large-cap equities. CDFIs lack consumer technology. Crowdfunding platforms skip due diligence. We are the first firm to combine hyperlocal community research, accessible technology, and institutional-grade investment methodology in one integrated platform.

Founded in 2025 and headquartered in Newark, NJ — we begin where impact is needed most, then scale.

Our Model

Five-step circular economy framework.

Traditional grants create one-time impact. Our circular model ensures capital keeps working within the community — multiplying returns with every cycle and creating self-sustaining economic ecosystems that compound over time.

01

Engage & Educate

Partner with local leaders, residents, and stakeholders to understand real community needs before a single dollar is deployed.

02

Map Resources

Assess community resource flows, gaps, and opportunities through proprietary hyperlocal research methodology.

03

Build Networks

Create local partnerships for resource sharing, cooperation, and sustained economic momentum across neighborhoods.

04

Implement Sustainability

Fund recycling, conservation, and waste reduction programs that permanently lower community costs.

05

Create Jobs & Growth

Support circular economy enterprises and workforce development for lasting, compounding community prosperity.
